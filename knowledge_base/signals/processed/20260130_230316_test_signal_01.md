# Distress Signal: WVU Budget Crisis

**Institution:** West Virginia University  
**Severity:** High  
**Issue:** Budget shortfall impacting staffing and academic programs.

## Summary
WVU has reported a significant budget gap. Immediate action is required to stabilize operations, mitigate layoffs, and protect core programs.

## Indicators
- Emergency board meetings
- Hiring freeze
- Program consolidation discussions
- Reported shortfall of $45M

## Recommended Actions
1. Initiate rapid financial audit.
2. Engage state funding partners.
3. Develop contingency staffing plans.
